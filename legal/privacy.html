Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Allure ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the Allure mobile application. By creating an account or using Allure, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, date of birth, and email address when you create an account
  • Profile photos and a profile picture you upload
  • Bio, interests, and personal details you fill in (height, zodiac, goals, etc.)
  • Messages you send to other users through the in-app chat
  • Subscription and payment information (processed by Apple; we do not store card numbers)

Information collected automatically:

  • Face scan image and biometric-derived attractiveness score ("Allure Score"), processed on-device and stored as a numeric value only
  • Last-active timestamps used to determine whether you are currently online
  • Device push-notification token for delivering match and message alerts
  • Basic device and OS information for crash reporting and compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and profile
  • To calculate your Allure Score and assign you to a compatibility tier
  • To display your profile to other users and show you potential matches
  • To deliver messages, match notifications, and in-app alerts
  • To verify subscription status and unlock Allure+ or Allure++ features
  • To detect and prevent fraud, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To improve the app through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Face Scan & Biometric Data

Allure uses your device camera to perform a one-time face scan that produces your Allure Score. The scan is processed locally on your device using on-device machine learning models. We do not upload raw facial images from the scan to our servers. Only the resulting numeric score and assigned tier are stored in your profile.

Your Allure Score is permanent after the first scan and cannot be re-scanned except through the designated "Rescan My Face" option where available. By using the face scan feature, you consent to this processing as described.

4. Photos & Media

Profile photos and gallery images you upload are stored securely in our cloud storage (Supabase Storage) and are visible to other Allure users. Photos you delete are removed from public access, though backup copies may persist in our systems for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other users: Your profile (name, age, photos, bio, interests, score) is shown to users in your match pool
  • Service providers: Supabase (database and storage), Apple (authentication, push notifications, in-app purchases), and Anthropic (AI-powered message suggestions — message context is sent to Anthropic's API and subject to their privacy policy)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to you

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business purposes (e.g., resolving disputes). Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction: You can view and edit your profile information at any time within the app
  • Deletion: You may delete your account from the app settings, which will trigger deletion of your personal data
  • Push notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings
  • Data portability: You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at the address below
  • California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data)

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure cloud infrastructure, and access controls to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

9. Children's Privacy

Allure is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete the account and associated data promptly. If you believe a minor is using Allure, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

Allure integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Apple — authentication, push notifications, and in-app purchases (apple.com/legal/privacy)
  • Anthropic — AI message suggestions via Claude API (anthropic.com/privacy)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via in-app notice or email at least 7 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Allure after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
